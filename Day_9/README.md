# Day 9 : 

## Conceptual codes to understand inheritance , encapsulation & abstraction in python  
---

## Encapsulation : 

- Encapsulation is considered as binding characteristics(variables) & behaviours(methods) together .
- To bind characteristics & behaviours we have to design class.

---

## Inheritance : 

- Inheritance means reusability.
- By using inheritance one can aquire all the characteristics & behaviours of another class.

---

## Abstraction : 

- Abstraction is considered as hiding something from outside entity.
